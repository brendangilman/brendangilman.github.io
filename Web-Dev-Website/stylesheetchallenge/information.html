What is the Style Sheet Challege?

What is this?

We make Webpages!

This challenge is actually is more of a compitition. A compitition held once a year, to find the best designed webpage. There is only a 24 hour window to design the best webpage.

Who are we?

We are a group of UI/UX designers!

After the 24 hours are up we review all the submitions and choose our top choices of best designs. We use a simple rubric to decide which one are the 'best'. We choose the top 3 winners and give them a spotlight on our site.
